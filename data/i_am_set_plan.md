# I AM set — multi-dimension build-out plan

Series `i-am` (John's 7 "I AM" sayings). Per the standing direction
(`multi-dimension-per-topic`): each saying → 2–3 distinct faithful truths,
each pinned to a verse. **Non-negotiable: Bible-driven + evangelical doctrine.**
Freshness in the entry-point only; orthodoxy in claim + landing.

Status key: ✅ shipped · 🔶 in queue

---

## 1. I AM the Bread of Life — John 6:35 (refs: John 6, Exodus 16)
- 🔶 **(a) The hunger bread can't fill** — the ache nothing satisfies points to a Person; *"shall never hunger... never thirst."*
- 🔶 **(b) Manna fulfilled** — fathers ate manna and died; *"if any man eat of this bread, he shall live for ever"* (6:51). Type → fulfillment.
- 🔶 **(c) The welcome that never turns you away** — *"him that cometh to me I will in no wise cast out"* (6:37). Assurance.

## 2. I AM the Light of the World — John 8:12
- ✅ **(a) The light you can stand in** — shipped as **#31** (honest woman-scene + pillar-of-fire woven in; full verse incl. *"light of life"*).
- 🔶 **(b) Light enacted on the man born blind** — John 9; *"one thing I know, that, whereas I was blind, now I see"* (9:25). Sight given, not earned.
- 🔶 **(c) Pillar of fire / lead-through-the-dark** — Tabernacles lamps recall the exodus pillar; the Light goes *before* you through your wilderness. (only if distinct enough from #31)

## 3. I AM the Door — John 10:9
- ✅ **(a) The invitation** — shipped **#32 The Door Was a Body** (open door, come in as you are; saved/safe/fed/pasture).
- ✅ **(b) The shepherd in the gap** — shipped **#33 The Shepherd In The Gap** (His body in the gap, the wolf comes first; folklore = accepted devotional latitude, core grounded in 10:11).

## 4. I AM the Good Shepherd — John 10:11 (refs: Psalm 23, Ezekiel 34)
- 🔶 **(a) Lays down His life** — *"the good shepherd giveth his life for the sheep"* vs the hireling who flees (10:11–13). Willing death.
- 🔶 **(b) Knows His own by name** — *"I call mine own sheep by name"* (10:3,14). Personal, sought, not a crowd.
- 🔶 **(c) Other sheep / one fold** — *"them also I must bring... one fold, and one shepherd"* (10:16). The gospel goes wide. (or Ezek 34: God Himself comes because the shepherds failed.)

## 5. I AM the Resurrection and the Life — John 11:25 (Lazarus)
- 🔶 **(a) Death is not the end** — *"though he were dead, yet shall he live"* (11:25). Hope at the grave.
- 🔶 **(b) Jesus wept** — *"Jesus wept"* (11:35); He doesn't despise your grief even knowing the ending. Compassion.
- 🔶 **(c) Believest thou this?** — *"believest thou this?"* (11:26). Resurrection is a Person to trust now, not just a future event. Viewer-turn.

## 6. I AM the Way, the Truth, and the Life — John 14:6
- 🔶 **(a) The only way is mercy** — *"no man cometh unto the Father, but by me"*; exclusivity = a way was *made*.
- 🔶 **(b) I go to prepare a place** — *"I go to prepare a place for you"* (14:2–3). The homecoming; He comes back to take you there.
- 🔶 **(c) Seen me, seen the Father** — *"he that hath seen me hath seen the Father"* (14:9). Want to know what God is like? Look at Jesus.

## 7. I AM the True Vine — John 15:1,5
- 🔶 **(a) Abide, don't strive** — *"without me ye can do nothing"* (15:5). Fruit comes from connection, not effort.
- 🔶 **(b) The pruning** — *"every branch that beareth fruit, he purgeth it"* (15:2). Why the painful seasons. The loving cut.
- 🔶 **(c) Friendship & full joy** — *"ye are my friends"* / *"that your joy might be full"* (15:11,15). Abiding ends in friendship.
