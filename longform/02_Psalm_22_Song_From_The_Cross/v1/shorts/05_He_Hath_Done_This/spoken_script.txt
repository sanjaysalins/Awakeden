A psalm's last line and Jesus' last breath in John close on the very same note: it is done. The psalm closes looking ahead — to God's saving work, accomplished, and told to a people not yet born. Its final line: "…that he hath done this." Done. And as Jesus hung dying, John records His final word: "It is finished". Different words, in different tongues, a thousand years apart — but the same note: a finished work. So what's left for you to finish? Nothing — only Someone to come home to: the One who said it is done.
