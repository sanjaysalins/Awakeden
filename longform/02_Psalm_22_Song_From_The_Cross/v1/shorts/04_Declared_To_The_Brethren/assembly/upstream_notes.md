## Standing 'design for the cut' rules

- Hero scene = the one most on-thread image (here the kiss); it bookends start+end, so render it with a clean, iconic, near-still composition.
- Every clip will likely be sped 1.2-2.2x: favour ONE dominant motion, avoid busy multi-action that turns to mush when accelerated.
- Tiny narration beats (a one-line aside, a single quoted phrase) deserve a purpose-built ~2s macro insert, not a full 10s scene.
- Front-load the emotional payload (the tear, the embrace) in the first half of the clip so head-biased trims keep it.
