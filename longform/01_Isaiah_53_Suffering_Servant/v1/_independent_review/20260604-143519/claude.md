- ⚠️ **Watch: "The arm of the LORD… is not a thing. It is a person… His name is Jesus."** (M7). This personifies "the arm of the LORD" — which in plain reading is God's *saving strength/power* — and identifies it as the person of Christ. It's defensible (John 12:38 applies Isa 53:1 to Jesus, and the Servant *is* the saving arm), so it's within orthodox typology, not invented doctrine. But it IS the script's biggest homiletical leap. Worth a conscious sign-off, not a silent pass.
