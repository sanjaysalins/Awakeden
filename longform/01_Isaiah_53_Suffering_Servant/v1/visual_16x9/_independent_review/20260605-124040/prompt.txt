## 0. The production loop (binding for every redone scene)

1. **NARRATION** — what is this beat actually saying?
2. **MOTION** — what must the viewer SEE move to carry that?
3. **FIRST FRAME** — what still lets that motion happen on top of it?
4. **ELEMENTS** — what must ALREADY be in the still (the lamb on the rope, the hand on
   the chariot, the dove mid-air) so the video animates what's there — never invents it?
→ then animate ONLY pre-placed elements, lock everything else, and **QC the WHOLE clip,
not just the last frame.**

**Hero-still bar:** one clear focal subject readable in <1s, strong chiaroscuro value
contrast, a scale/depth cue, and it must *tell more* than it shows. **Epic-open bar
(S1):** a first frame that makes a scrolling viewer stop and decide to wait.
