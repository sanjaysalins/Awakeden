You can be completely full and still feel empty. Fridge stocked, day went fine — and something deeper than your stomach is still hungry. That ache isn't a flaw to fix. It's a signpost. You were built to be filled by Someone, not something. Hear Him: "I am the bread of life: he that cometh to me shall never hunger; and he that believeth on me shall never thirst." Not less hungry. Never. So you keep reaching for the next thing — the next plate, the next purchase, the next person — asking created things to do what only Christ can do. Stop feeding a soul-hunger with things that spoil. Come to the Bread of life — not a snack for the moment, but the life your soul was made for.
