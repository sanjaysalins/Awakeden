## RED-TEAM (my own adversarial pass before the panel)

**1. Doctrine: tension music must never touch the conviction/CTA.** The engine's locked
rule is grace-anchored conviction ‚Äî NO fear/pressure framing. So `tension_*` (#13/#14)
are for **narrative villainy only** (betrayal, storm, the mob), and are **banned** from
the Conviction and Landing beats. Conviction = `tender_*` / `sacred_*` / `lonely_*`. This
is now a hard usage rule in the catalogue, not a suggestion.

**2. The "two music" clash (just hit on the Well clip).** Two melodic beds at once fight
(different keys/builds). Rule: **one melodic bed per clip.** The only safe layering partner
is a `glory_*` pad/drone (#15/#16) ‚Äî they're deliberately melody-free and rhythm-free so
they can sit *under* a melodic bed without clashing. Designed that way on purpose.

**3. Mood overlap (sacred / awe / glory / tender).** Genuine risk of 4 samey tracks. They
are deliberately separated: **sacred** = devotional melody w/ grace arc; **awe** = mounting
crescendo (it *moves*); **glory** = static atmosphere, no melody; **tender** = warm human
compassion, smaller. If two takes come back indistinguishable, cut one and widen the other.

**4. Suno ignores negatives.** "no drums" is unreliable. Mitigation: the *genre framing*
does the work (chamber / ambient / sacred orchestral rarely add a beat); reject any take
that comes back with a drum kit. Tension/urgent tracks intentionally allow percussion.

**5. Stealth vocals.** Even with Instrumental ON, Suno sometimes adds "aah" vocal pads.
A wordless pad is borderline; for beds that sit UNDER narration, reject vocal takes
(voice-on-voice muddies the mix). The `awe`/`glory` choir-pad prompts are the main risk ‚Äî
audition those carefully.

**6. 60s usability.** Very slow beds (~48‚Äì55 bpm) may not present a full arc inside a 59s
window. Mitigation: we don't need the whole track ‚Äî `enhance.py` trims to any window and
picks the in-point, so a 2‚Äì3 min Suno track gives several usable 60s sections.

**7. Loudness.** Suno masters hot (~-8 LUFS). Not a problem: `ingest.py` measures each
track and the downstream mix attenuates + sidechain-ducks under the voice (proven on Well).

**8. Coverage gaps considered.** Childlike/whimsical (rare in this content ‚Äî skip for now),
courtroom/legal-conviction (covered by tension narrative + the doctrine rule above keeps it
off the CTA), worship-anthem with vocals (out of scope ‚Äî beds are instrumental). Catalogue
is intentionally 10 moods; expand later from real usage, not speculation.
