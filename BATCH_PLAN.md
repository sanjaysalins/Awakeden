# BATCH PRODUCTION PLAN — passion/cross slate (v0, 2026-06-06)

The agreed way to run a batch of episodes. Strategy memory: `shorts-longform-funnel`.

## Philosophy (locked)
- **Shorts = the snack / hook** (60s, viral, one sharp truth → discovery).
- **Long-forms = the meat & potatoes** (16:9, ~6-8 min, comprehensive, deeply
  researched, KJV-grounded, steel-manned → depth/retention).
- One body of work at two depths: the short is the trailer, the long-form is the
  film. The long BACKS UP every claim the short makes; the short POINTS TO the long.
- **Write the long-form FIRST** (research foundation); shorts are distilled cuts from it.

## Reuse constraints (honest)
- Reuse is bounded to **same-format, thread-NEUTRAL** plates: long↔long (16:9) and
  short↔short (9:16); NOT long↔short (different crop). Story-specific stills stay with
  their episode (topical-fit gate). Jesus-face refs cross everything for consistency.
- Don't over-reuse → sameness. Keep signature scenes per episode.
- The passion/cross slate maximizes overlap: lamb, blood, cross/lifted-up, wilderness,
  tomb, risen Christ recur across nearly every episode.
