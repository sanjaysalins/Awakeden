### #02 — *The Mockers’ Words*
**CTA:** *"He stayed, for you."*  
**Flow lines (the real problem):** *"They jeered that He couldn't save Himself, and they were right about one thing: He wouldn't."*

**(b) SPECIAL CHECK — FAIL.** This is a **thread swap and a regression.**

The middle builds the Ps 22:8 / Matt 27:43 spine: *"let him deliver him."* The closing pivot imports **Matt 27:42’s different taunt** (*"himself he cannot save"*) as unquoted paraphrase: *"couldn't save Himself."* That is not on the 22:8 thread; it is a second mockery the script never quoted. Your own locked #02 notes explicitly **cut** the 27:42 line for this reason.

Worse: *"and they were right about one thing"* still concedes to the mockers **before** the reversal lands. On first hearing, a zero-Bible listener hears: mockers said He couldn’t save Himself → narrator agrees they were right (about something) → then corrects. That is the exact structural hazard a prior panel flagged as near-heresy (conceding impotence). *"He wouldn't"* repairs it only if the listener survives the concession.

**CTA** *"He stayed, for you"* is punchy but **thin**: action, not Person; and it **duplicates #01’s** *"for you"* close — weakens the “8 varied forms” claim.

**Verdict on CTA + flow:** Revise. Keep the deliver taunt; drop the 27:42 paraphrase and *"they were right"* frame.

### #03 — *The Forsaken Cry*

- **Flow:** Earned by *"He was forsaken so that you never will be."* One of the strongest in the set.

**Verdict:** Pass.

### #04 — *Declared To The Brethren*
**CTA:** *"The risen Christ looked at the world that nailed Him down and called it brethren — and there's room in that word for you."*

**Doctrinal overreach.** Hebrews 2:11 (just quoted) says He is *"not ashamed to call **them** brethren"* — **them** = *"they who are sanctified"* (Heb 2:11 context), not the unrepentant world that crucified Him. The psalm’s *"my brethren"* is the risen Christ’s familial declaration to those brought near — not a blanket renaming of His executioners.

*"there's room in that word for you"* softens it as invitation, but the preceding clause **overstates**: it sounds like universal familial status already granted to *"the world that nailed Him."* That blurs election/response and inches toward universalism-on-first-hearing.

**Flow:** The quote says *"them"*; the CTA immediately universalizes to *"the world."* That’s a logic jump, not a landing.

**Verdict:** Revise. Scope *brethren* to those who come / are sanctified — don’t call the nailing world *brethren*.

---

### #05 — *He Hath Done This*
**CTA:** *"So if He has already said it is done, what are you still trying to finish?"*  
**Setup:** *"nothing left to earn, to prove, to add."*

**(a) SPECIAL CHECK — Conditional pass, clarity risk.**

In full context, the question is **anti-works**, not pro-works: it targets finishing what Christ already finished. That’s fresh form (not the banned bare *"will you trust Him?"*) and grace-anchored **if** the listener caught the prior sentence.

On **first hearing**, isolated: *"what are you still trying to finish?"* can scan as **accusation of incomplete spiritual effort** — guilt without the grace guard baked into the question itself. The close also **does not name Christ**; it points at the viewer’s activity.

**Verdict:** Revise lightly — keep the question form, embed the grace guard *in* the question or final beat.

### #06 — *The Ends Of The Earth*
**CTA:** *"That song began with one man dying alone and kept widening until it reached you; you are the ends of the earth it was singing toward."*  
**Bridge:** *"And 'the ends of the world' includes wherever you are, right now."*

**(c) SPECIAL CHECK — Poetic application, mild overreach.**

Ps 22:27’s thrust is **nations remembering and turning unto the LORD** — a conversion/worship motion. The CTA collapses that into **geographic identity**: *"you **are** the ends of the earth."* Defensible as *"wherever you are counts as ‘ends of the earth’"* (the bridge helps), but it **drops the psalm’s turning verb** and can sound like fulfilled destiny rather than *shall remember and turn*. Not heresy; slightly over-poetic.

**Christ:** Indirect — *"the song,"* *"one man dying"* — weaker Person-landing than #03, #07, #08.

**Verdict:** Caution / minor revise. Consider retaining *turn* language, not just *you are* identity.
